Goals

Improve Townsville Community Music Centre (TCMC) Business by getting on the internet to :

  1. Appeal viewers to know more about TCMC or Townsville Community Music Centre.
  2. Promote Townsville Community Music Centre(TCMC).
  3. Recruit new members
  4. Improve visitor and sales.
  5. Provide viewer of Townsville Community Music Centre (TCMC) History,News,contact,details,etc.
  6. Promote and let people know Event and photogallery of TCMC or Townsville Community Music Centre.
  7. Make TCMC or Townsville Community Music Center customer and client to Sign-up for event and get to know announcement faster from website.

Success Evaluation

The main success Evaluation here is :

  1. The Audience know TCMC or Townsville Community Music Centre purpose , News ,history,contact , and other details.
  2. Make viewer interested in TCMC or Townsville Community Music Centre.
  3. Increasing viewer interest to know TCMC or Townsville Community Music Centre more and sign up for event.
  4. Increasing audience or new customer visits.
  5. Increasing client and customer interest by seeing the TCMC activity from Photo Gallery.

Target Audience

The Target Audience is :

  1. Audience passionate and love any kind of Music genre.
  2. Audience like to hear and practice music.
  3. Young Audience that wanted to learn music and form group.
  4. Audience that looking for community to share and help others in any music genre.
  5. Teenager audience that likes hangout in crowd place with music atmosphere.
